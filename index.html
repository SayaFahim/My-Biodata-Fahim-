My Favourite Food

Asam Pedas Ayam

Asam Pedas Ayam has been my favorite food since I was a kid. It is renowned for its strong flavours, which combine the acidity of tamarind with the fiery heat of the chilli to create a meal that is both energising and soothing. Particularly for people who enjoy sour and spicy flavours, it is a favourite in Malaysian homes and eateries.

Ayam Masak Kicap

A popular and traditional Malaysian meal, Ayam Masak Kicap is renowned for its rich, savoury, and somewhat sweet flavour. "Ayam Masak Kicap" literally means "chicken cooked in soy sauce," which characterises the dish's main cooking technique and flavour.

Samyang Hot Chicken Carbonara

Samyang Hot Chicken Carbonara is a delicious and spicy noodle dish that has gained popularity for its unique blend of flavours. The creamy sauce combined with the spicy chicken creates a delightful experience for those who enjoy a kick in their meals.

My Favourite Drink

Milk Tea

A popular beverage used all around the world, especially in Asia, milk tea is made by combining tea with milk (or occasionally cream, condensed milk, or non-dairy substitutes) to produce a smooth, creamy, and reassuring beverage. It is a favourite in many cultures because of its adaptability, which enables it to be consumed hot or cold and can be customised with different kinds of tea, sweeteners, and other flavours.

Orange Juice

Due to its naturally sweet, tangy, and refreshing flavour, orange juice is one of the most popular and extensively consumed fruit juices worldwide. It is prepared by removing the liquid from the pulp of fresh oranges, usually by juicing them. It may be consumed as a pleasant beverage at any time of day or as a breakfast beverage.

Sirap Bandung

A popular beverage in Malaysia and Singapore, Sirap Bandung is sweet and refreshing. It is mostly made of condensed milk and rose syrup (sirap), giving it its characteristic pink hue and producing a delightfully sweet and creamy beverage.
